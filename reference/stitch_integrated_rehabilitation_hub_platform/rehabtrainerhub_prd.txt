# RehabTrainerHub Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview
* **Product Name:** RehabTrainerHub
* **Vision:** Extend professional rehabilitation resources from medical institutions to homes, providing a seamless, easy-to-use, and highly integrated portal for home rehabilitation mini-games.
* **Core Solution:** Establish a unified Hub website using a Monorepo architecture to integrate existing services (StrokeTrainer and VisionTrainer).

## 2. Target Audience
1. **Rehabilitation Clients:** Patients needing stroke or vision rehab at home. Interface must be intuitive with clear fonts and "foolproof" operations.
2. **Family/Caregivers:** Assisting with setup and games. Need clear guidance and educational info.
3. **Occupational Therapists:** Using this as a "home rehab prescription" tool for clients.

## 3. Functional Requirements
### 3.1 Main Site (RehabTrainerHub)
* **Landing Page:** Introduce platform philosophy, provide clear navigation to Stroke and Vision sub-systems.
* **Shared Navigation & Footer:** Consistent branding across all sub-apps.
* **Educational Info:** Purpose, target audience, and operation instructions for each game.

### 3.2 Sub-systems
* **StrokeTrainer:** Integrated stroke rehab functionality.
* **VisionTrainer:** Integrated vision rehab functionality.

## 4. Design Direction
* **Clarity & Accessibility:** Large typography, high contrast, and intuitive layouts suitable for elderly or visually impaired users.
* **Professional yet Warm:** A medical-grade feel that is welcoming and encouraging.
* **Consistent Branding:** Unified colors and components across the hub and all sub-games.
