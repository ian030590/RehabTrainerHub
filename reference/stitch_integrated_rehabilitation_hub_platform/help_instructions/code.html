How to Use RehabTrainerHub

Welcome to your recovery platform. This guide is designed to help patients and caregivers navigate the site easily and safely.

login

1. Getting Started

Begin by signing into your account. If you are a caregiver logging in for a patient, ensure you select the correct profile. Your dashboard will automatically load today's recommended exercises.

Tip: Keep your login details secure but easily accessible for daily use.

play_circle

2. Starting an Exercise

Navigate to "Stroke Recovery" or "Vision Training". Click on a card to open an exercise. Each module features a large video demonstration and clear text instructions.

Safety: Always perform exercises in a clear, well-lit space.

check_circle

3. Tracking Progress

After completing an exercise, click the "Complete" button. Your progress bar will update. Consistent daily practice is key to recovery.

support_agent

4. Need Help?

If an exercise feels too difficult, stop immediately. You can contact support or consult your clinical team through the platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need special equipment?

expand_more

Most exercises are designed to be done with minimal equipment (like a chair or a soft ball). If specific equipment is required, it will be clearly listed at the top of the exercise module.

How often should I practice?

expand_more

Your clinical team usually recommends a specific frequency. As a general guideline, 15-20 minutes of focused practice daily is better than one long session a week. Listen to your body and rest when fatigued.

What if I experience pain?

expand_more

Stop immediately. Rehabilitation exercises should not cause sharp or severe pain. Mild muscle fatigue is normal, but pain indicates you should stop and consult your medical provider.
