Tips for Success

For optimal recovery, consistency is key. Aim for 15-20 minutes of daily practice. Ensure you are seated in a stable, comfortable chair with your device placed securely on a flat surface at eye level to prevent neck strain. Stop immediately if you feel sharp pain or excessive fatigue.

Why Stroke Rehab at Home Matters

Neuroplasticity—the brain's ability to reorganize itself by forming new neural connections—is most active when exercises are performed with high repetition and consistency. While clinic visits are crucial, the bulk of recovery happens through daily practice at home. RehabTrainerHub bridges this gap, providing clinical-grade exercises designed to keep you engaged, track your progress, and support your journey towards independence.
