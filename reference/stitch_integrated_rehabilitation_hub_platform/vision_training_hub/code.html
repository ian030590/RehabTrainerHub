VisionTrainer Program

Understanding Visual Rehabilitation

visibility

Neuroplasticity

The brain's ability to reorganize itself by forming new neural connections. Consistent vision training helps rebuild visual processing pathways.

target

Oculomotor Control

Exercises focus on improving eye movements, such as smooth pursuits (following a moving target) and saccades (rapid shifts in focus).

contrast

Contrast Sensitivity

Enhancing the ability to distinguish between an object and its background, crucial for daily tasks in varying lighting conditions.

Environment Setup Guidelines

  • lightbulb
    Optimal Lighting Ensure the room is evenly lit. Avoid direct glare on the screen from windows or overhead lights.
  • straighten
    Screen Distance Position yourself approximately 20-24 inches (50-60 cm) from the monitor. Use the calibration tool before starting.
  • accessibility_new
    Posture & Comfort Sit comfortably with your head level. If you experience dizziness or fatigue, stop the exercise and rest.
